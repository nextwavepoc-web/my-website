Irregular Periods
Understanding your cycle
Your cycle is telling you something

Irregular periods are one of the most common concerns women come to us with, and one of the most under-investigated. A cycle that's unpredictable, painful, very heavy or barely there is worth looking into properly.

Signs we look for
  • ✓Cycles shorter than 21 days or longer than 35
  • ✓Missing periods for 3 or more months
  • ✓Bleeding that's extremely heavy or very light
  • ✓Significant PMS, mood shifts, or cramping
  • ✓Spotting between periods
Questions we'll explore with you
  • How long has your cycle been irregular?
  • Any recent changes in weight, stress, or sleep?
  • Have you had your thyroid or hormone levels checked before?
  • Are you on any contraception currently?

"I was told it was just stress for years. Turns out it was a hormonal imbalance nobody had tested for."

Many women wait years before getting a real answer. You don't have to.

PCOS
More than a diagnosis
PCOS is complex. Your care should be too.

PCOS is one of the most common hormonal conditions in women, yet most women receive generic advice. At Unlock, we look at your specific hormonal picture, not a standard protocol.

What we investigate
  • ✓Insulin resistance and blood sugar patterns
  • ✓Androgen levels causing acne, hair loss or growth
  • ✓Ovulation patterns and cycle regularity
  • ✓Gut health, inflammation and nutrient deficiencies
  • ✓Mood, energy and sleep disruption
Questions we'll explore with you
  • When were you diagnosed, and what tests were done?
  • What symptoms bother you most right now?
  • Have you tried any treatment or lifestyle changes so far?
  • Are you thinking about fertility in the near future?

"I was just told to lose weight. Nobody looked at my insulin, my gut, or my actual hormones."

PCOS is not one condition. Your plan should reflect your version of it.

Hormone Guidance
Hormonal health
Hormones affect everything. We take them seriously.

Fatigue, brain fog, mood swings, unexplained weight changes, low libido, hair thinning - these are not "just getting older." They're signals worth investigating.

What we assess
  • ✓Full hormonal panel including oestrogen, progesterone, testosterone
  • ✓Thyroid function, including T3, T4 and TSH
  • ✓Cortisol and stress hormone patterns
  • ✓Nutritional deficiencies linked to hormonal imbalance
Questions we'll explore with you
  • Which symptoms are affecting your daily life most?
  • Have you had hormonal blood work done in the last year?
  • Do symptoms follow a pattern across your cycle?
  • Any family history of thyroid or hormonal conditions?

"I was exhausted, gaining weight and told everything was 'normal.' Unlock found my thyroid was underactive."

If you feel off but can't explain it, that's worth investigating.
