Irregular Periods
Understanding your cycle
Your cycle is telling you something

Irregular periods are one of the most common concerns women come to us with, and one of the most under-investigated. A cycle that's unpredictable, painful, very heavy or barely there is worth looking into properly.

Signs we look for
  • ✓Cycles shorter than 21 days or longer than 35
  • ✓Missing periods for 3 or more months
  • ✓Bleeding that's extremely heavy or very light
  • ✓Significant PMS, mood shifts, or cramping
  • ✓Spotting between periods
Questions we'll explore with you
  • How long has your cycle been irregular?
  • Any recent changes in weight, stress, or sleep?
  • Have you had your thyroid or hormone levels checked before?
  • Are you on any contraception currently?

"I was told it was just stress for years. Turns out it was a hormonal imbalance nobody had tested for."

Many women wait years before getting a real answer. You don't have to.

PCOS
Polycystic Ovary Syndrome
PCOS is more than a hormone problem

PCOS affects insulin, androgens, ovulation, mood, skin, and gut health. Most women with PCOS have only ever been given one piece of that picture. We look at all of it.

Signs we look for
  • ✓Irregular or absent periods
  • ✓Unexplained weight gain or difficulty losing weight
  • ✓Acne, hair thinning, or excess facial hair
  • ✓Mood swings, anxiety, or fatigue
  • ✓Difficulty conceiving
Questions we'll explore with you
  • Have you had an ultrasound or hormonal blood panel done?
  • Which symptoms are affecting your daily life the most?
  • Has anyone looked at your insulin or thyroid markers?
  • What have you already tried, and what happened?

"I was diagnosed at 22 and told to lose weight. Nobody explained what was actually happening inside my body."

A PCOS plan should be built around your specific hormonal profile, not a generic protocol.

Free first call. No commitment needed.

Trying to Conceive
Fertility support
Trying to conceive is more than tracking ovulation

Fertility prep involves your hormones, nutrition, cycle health, and your partner's health too. We look at the full picture before you start trying, or to understand why it hasn't happened yet.

Areas we assess
  • ✓Cycle regularity and ovulation patterns
  • ✓Hormonal baseline (AMH, FSH, thyroid, progesterone)
  • ✓Nutritional status and deficiencies that affect conception
  • ✓Unexplained infertility or previous pregnancy loss
  • ✓Lifestyle factors that impact egg quality
Questions we'll explore with you
  • How long have you been trying to conceive?
  • Have you had any fertility investigations so far?
  • Do you have regular, predictable cycles?
  • Are you open to nutritional support alongside medical guidance?

"After 14 months of trying, I finally understood what my body actually needed. It wasn't just about timing."

Whether you're just starting or have been trying for a while, there's always more to understand.

Hormone Guidance
Hormonal health
Your hormones affect everything

Fatigue, mood swings, weight changes, brain fog, low libido — these are often hormonal. But standard blood tests miss a lot, and most GPs don't have time to dig deeper. We do.

Signs we look for
  • ✓Persistent fatigue that sleep doesn't fix
  • ✓Mood swings, anxiety, or low mood tied to your cycle
  • ✓Unexplained weight gain, especially around the middle
  • ✓Brain fog, poor concentration, or memory issues
  • ✓Hair loss, skin changes, or low libido
Questions we'll explore with you
  • Which symptoms are most affecting your quality of life?
  • Have you had a full hormonal panel done recently?
  • Do your symptoms follow a pattern through the month?
  • Any family history of thyroid conditions or hormonal issues?

"I was told my results were 'normal'. But I didn't feel normal. Unlock found what everyone else had missed."

Normal ranges aren't always optimal. We look for what's right for you.

Free first call. No commitment needed.

Nutrition Support
Nutritional health
Food is medicine, if you know how to use it

Women's nutritional needs shift constantly through the cycle, through hormonal conditions, and through life stages. Generic advice rarely fits. Our nutritionists work from your actual results, not generic plans.

Areas we cover
  • ✓Nutrition for PCOS, thyroid, and hormonal balance
  • ✓Gut health and its connection to hormones
  • ✓Deficiency correction (iron, B12, vitamin D, magnesium)
  • ✓Eating patterns that support cycle health
  • ✓Supplement guidance based on test results
Questions we'll explore with you
  • Have you had any blood tests or nutritional panels done?
  • Are there specific symptoms you're hoping nutrition can help?
  • Any dietary restrictions or preferences to work around?
  • Are you managing a condition like PCOS or thyroid alongside this?

"I didn't know what I was deficient in. Once I found out, everything started to make sense."

Supplements without testing is guesswork. We start with your actual numbers.
